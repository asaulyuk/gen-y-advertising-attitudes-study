# Gen Y Advertising Attitudes Study

## Overview
This project is a quantitative marketing research study examining Generation Y attitudes and beliefs toward advertising. The objective was to analyze how demographic factors and advertising-related beliefs influence overall attitudes toward advertising and translate findings into actionable marketing insights.

## Research Methodology
- Survey-based quantitative research design  
- Sample size: 266 respondents  
- Cross-sectional, self-administered questionnaire  
- Data analyzed using SPSS (descriptive statistics, t-tests, ANOVA, and regression analysis)

## Files Included
- **Gen_Y_Advertising_Attitudes_Quant_Study_Asauliuk_Marko_2025.pdf** – Final presentation summarizing research questions, methods, key findings, and managerial implications
